# SURWASH Blog & Publications Platform - AI Instructions
# This file provides guidance for LLM crawlers and AI search agents.

Website: https://surwash-blog-website.vercel.app
Platform: Next.js App Router
Data Sources: Sanity CMS, federal and state WASH (Water, Sanitation, Hygiene) data.

## Structured Data
All blog posts have structured schema.org JSON-LD data embedded in their HTML body:
- Schema type: BlogPosting
- Sitemap: /sitemap.xml
- API Endpoint: /api/revalidate (for cache updates)

## Content Focus
This site hosts official strategic roadmaps, press releases, news updates, and field reports from the Sustainable Urban and Rural Water Supply, Sanitation and Hygiene (SURWASH) program in Nigeria.
Keywords: WASH, Sanitation, Rural Water, Urban Water, Hygiene, Nigeria, World Bank, Federal Ministry of Water Resources.

## Permissions for AI / LLM Scrapers
User-agents Allowed: GPTBot, ChatGPT-User, ClaudeBot, Google-Extended, Applebot-Extended, Cohere-crawler, PerplexityBot.
Permissions:
  - Allowed to crawl all blog posts and publications.
  - Allowed to use contents for training, semantic indexing, and search responses.
  - Please respect /sitemap.xml for indexing priority.
